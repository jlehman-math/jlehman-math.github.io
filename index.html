About Me

I am a third year graduate student at Notre Dame. My advisor is Nick Salter. In May of 2023 I graduated from the University of Auckland, New Zealand. In my spare time, I enjoy exploring wherever I am by bike.

Interests

I enjoy thinking about interactions between complex algebraic geometry and low dimensional topology. Recently, I've been thinking about smooth monodromy groups of surfaces in four manifolds.
